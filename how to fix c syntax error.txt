gcc -std=c2x -Wall -lm -o hello hello.c
1. 预处理 (Preprocessing)   hello.c → hello.i
2. 编译 (Compilation)       hello.i → hello.s
3. 汇编 (Assembly)          hello.s → hello.o
4. 链接 (Linking)           hello.o + libm → hello
